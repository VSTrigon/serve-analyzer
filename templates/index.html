ATP BENCHMARKS
Elbow angle at contact
172°
Range: 160° – 180°
Nearly full arm extension at contact point. The straighter the arm, the more racket head speed is transferred to the ball.
Shoulder angle peak
175°
Range: 165° – 185°
Full arm elevation at the peak of the trophy pose. Drives the kinetic chain from legs through core to racket.
Shoulder tilt at contact
3.5
Range: 1 – 6
Slight positive tilt (right shoulder higher than left) at contact. Indicates proper shoulder-over-shoulder rotation through the serve.
